    About Us

    Adorning your event delightfully is our expertise.

    Dimmydecor_events

    DIMMY DECOR is a highly reputable event management company registered under the Cooperate Affairs Commission of Nigeria.

    DIMMY DECOR events’ expertise in event management comprise; Event planning, Event execution, Event decoration, Vendor sourcing and Event management training. We have been privileged to delightfully managed events such as wedding parties, musical concerts, Annual general parties for various organizations, church anniversaries, House warming, Project commissioning, Dinner parties, conferences, family reunion parties, Bachelor and Bachelorette parties, Birth anniversaries, Burial wake-keep and party reception, Dignitary grand reception parties.

    AIM
    • To help our client maximize budget thereby making the event economically reasonable.

    • To help our client identify tangible and intangible goals of the events thereby making the event delightsome for client/celebrant and guest/participants.
